# investment-portfolio
Quantitative investment analysis projects — asset allocation, options pricing, and algo trading

A collection of quantitative finance projects covering asset allocation and options pricing.

---

## Projects

### 1. Asset Allocation & Portfolio Optimization
> Mean-variance optimization with efficient frontier visualization, incorporating a macro framework inspired by All-Weather portfolio thinking.

**Tools:** Python, NumPy, pandas, matplotlib, scipy

### 2. Options Pricing & Greeks
> Black-Scholes pricing model with interactive Greeks visualization (Delta, Gamma, Vega, Theta). Explores how implied volatility affects option value.

**Tools:** Python, NumPy, matplotlib

Incoming MMA student at Queen's University Smith School of Business. Previously interned in venture capital due diligence and pension system operations. Interested in quantitative investment analysis and insurance asset management.
